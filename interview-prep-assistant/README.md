## Description
The Interview Prep Assistant generates relevant practice questions based on job titles and focus areas. Perfect for job seekers looking to prepare for technical, behavioral, and system design interviews.

- üéØ **Role-Specific Questions**: Customized questions based on job titles

- üì± **Easy to Use**: Simple interface for quick preparation sessions

## Focus Areas Supported
- **Technical Questions**: Problem-solving, tools, technologies, best practices
- **Behavioral Questions**: Teamwork, challenges, leadership, mistakes
- **System Design**: Scalability, architecture, trade-offs, monitoring

## How to Use
1. Enter your target job title (e.g., Frontend Engineer, Product Manager)
2. Select your focus area (Technical/Behavioral/System Design)
3. Click "Generate 5 Questions"
4. Practice answering each question out loud
5. Repeat with different focus areas for comprehensive prep
