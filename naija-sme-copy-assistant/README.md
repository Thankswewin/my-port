## Description
The Naija SME Copy Assistant is a specialized AI tool that generates engaging social media captions specifically designed for Nigerian small businesses. It understands the local context, culture, and marketing language that resonates with Nigerian audiences.

1. Select your business type
2. Choose your target platform
3. Describe your offer or promotion
4. Generate 3 unique caption ideas
5. Copy and use across your social media
